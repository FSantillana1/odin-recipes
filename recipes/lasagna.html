World's Best Lasagna

Lasagna sagna ZA!

Making a lasagna is time consuming, but the results are worth it. With a few ingredients you can make a dish that will position yourself as the top dog at the cookout! So let's get barking!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the Noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before eating.
